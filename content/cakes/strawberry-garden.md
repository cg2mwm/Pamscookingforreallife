## About This Cake

Light, fresh, and beautiful — this cake celebrates the best of summer. Made with real strawberries slow-cooked into a jammy compote, not artificial flavoring.

### Seasonal Note

Best enjoyed April through September when strawberries are at peak flavor. We source locally when possible.

### Storage

Keep refrigerated. Best enjoyed within 2 days of pickup/delivery.
